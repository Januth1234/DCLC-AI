# DCLC Usage Policy

## Content Policy

DCLC does not restrict content. By default, it can generate explicit or adult content when prompted. There is no built-in content filter.

- **Unfiltered mode (default)**: All prompts and outputs are allowed. Explicit content is permitted.
- **Filtered mode**: Optional. User enables in Settings. May restrict certain content types.

By using DCLC, you acknowledge that you are responsible for your use of the model and its outputs, including compliance with applicable laws in your jurisdiction. The model may generate explicit content.

## Filter Toggle

- **OFF (default)**: Unfiltered. Raw model output.
- **ON**: Optional filter active. User enables in Settings.
